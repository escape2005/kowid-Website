Internships: Learn. Contribute. Grow.

Be part of building Kowid from the ground up

Be Part of Building Kowid

Our interns aren't just helping—they're co-creating foundational education's future.

Research & Analysis

  • Conduct user research with teachers & students
  • Analyze learning data and trends
  • Research EdTech competitive landscapes

Design & Prototyping

  • Design wireframes and user flows
  • Prototype features and test concepts
  • Create visual content and brand materials

Content & Communication

  • Write content, case studies, blog posts
  • Develop curriculum ideas
  • Support social media and outreach

Partnerships & Strategy

  • Outreach to schools and NGO partners
  • Contribute to product strategy
  • Help build the roadmap

What You'll Learn

How early-stage startups operate
User research and product discovery methods
Education technology and learning science
How to turn insights into action
Cross-functional collaboration
Real-world problem-solving in complex domains

What We're Looking For

Students or recent graduates passionate about education & tech
Curious learners who ask great questions
Self-starters who take initiative
Empathetic thinkers who care about social impact
Collaborative team players who share knowledge generously
Committed contributors (10-20 hours/week minimum)

We welcome interns from diverse backgrounds—engineering, design, education, social sciences, business, and beyond.

What We Offer

✓ Real Responsibility

Your work will shape Kowid's direction

✓ Mentorship

Learn from experienced founders and collaborators

✓ Exposure

Engage with educators, researchers, and industry experts

✓ Community

Join a team of passionate, mission-driven people

✓ Flexibility

Remote-friendly, with flexible hours

✓ Stipend

Modest financial support based on contribution level

✓ Certificate & Recommendations

Recognition for your work

✓ Potential for Full-Time

Top performers may transition to core team roles

Our Expectations

  • Commitment to learning and growth
  • Ownership of assigned projects
  • Proactive communication
  • Openness to feedback
  • Alignment with Kowid's mission and values

Duration

Internships typically run for 2-6 months, with the possibility of extension.

How to Apply

Ready to contribute to something meaningful?

Send the following to interns@kowid.in

  • Your resume/CV
  • A short note (200-300 words) explaining:
    • Why you're interested in Kowid
    • What skills/perspective you bring
    • What you hope to learn
  • Any relevant work samples (optional but appreciated)

Rolling Applications: We review applications on a rolling basis. Apply anytime—we're always looking for great people.
