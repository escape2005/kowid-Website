The Challenge We're Exploring

Understanding the learning crisis in foundational education

India's Learning Crisis in Numbers

Enrollment is up. But learning? That's a different story.

Silent Learning Gaps

  • 50% of Class 5 students can't read Class 2 texts
  • Math skills lag even further
  • Gaps compound year after year

No Personalization

  • 40+ students per class
  • Vastly different learning levels
  • Teachers lack time & tools

Too Late to Help

  • Issues identified years too late
  • Parents lack classroom visibility
  • Gaps already widened

Access Inequality

  • Rural schools under-resourced
  • Quality support unavailable
  • Digital tools not India-ready

Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Most edtech focuses on content delivery or test prep—not early detection and personalized intervention at the foundational level.

There's room for something different. Something that works with the system, supports teachers, and meets children where they are.
