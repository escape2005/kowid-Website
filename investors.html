For Investors: An Early-Stage Opportunity

Join us in building a solution to India's foundational learning crisis

Investing in Impact

Early-stage. High ambition. Clear direction.

🚧 Transparency First

We're at the idea stage—no product, no users, no revenue. Just research, interns, and conviction.

The Problem is Massive

250M+ children in foundational education. Half struggle with basic literacy and numeracy. This isn't just educational—it's economic, social, and profoundly human.

The Market is Ready

Government prioritization (NEP 2020, foundational literacy initiatives)
Growing edtech adoption post-COVID
Increasing parental awareness and investment
Technology infrastructure improving across urban and rural India
AI/ML making personalization feasible at scale

Our Approach is Different

Early Detection

Identifying learning gaps before they compound

Personalization

Adapting to each child's unique needs

Teacher Empowerment

Supporting educators, not replacing them

Accessibility

Designed for under-resourced communities
