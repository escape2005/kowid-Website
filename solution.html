Our Vision: What We're Building Toward

Exploring solutions that make learning accessible and personalized for every child

🚧 We're Still Exploring

No finished product yet. Just a clear direction informed by research and guided by principles.

The Concept

An AI-powered platform that identifies learning gaps early and delivers personalized support for literacy and numeracy.

What We're Researching

🔍 Gap Detection

How to spot learning gaps in reading and math at the earliest stages

🧠 AI-Powered Paths

How AI can help teachers create personalized learning journeys

🎮 Engagement

Making learning age-appropriate, fun, and effective

🌐 Accessibility

Designing for low-connectivity and multilingual contexts

Our Principles

✅ Accessible

Works in low-resource settings

✅ Teacher-Supportive

Empowers, doesn't replace

✅ Child-Centered

Adapts to each learner

✅ Data-Informed

Insights drive outcomes

✅ Evidence-Based

Grounded in learning science

✅ Transparent

Honest about what we know

What We're NOT Doing

❌ Building another content library
❌ Replacing teachers with AI
❌ Claiming to have all the answers

Our Roadmap

Phase 1: Discovery & Research (0–3 Months)

  • Conduct user research with teachers, parents, and students
  • Map the foundational learning landscape in India
  • Study existing solutions and identify gaps
  • Develop early concept prototypes and wireframes
  • Build partnerships with pilot schools and NGOs

Phase 2: Validation & Concept Testing (3–6 Months)

  • Test early prototypes with small groups
  • Gather feedback on usability and relevance
  • Iterate rapidly based on real-world use
  • Validate core assumptions
  • Build relationships with education experts

Phase 3: Early Product Build & Pilot (6–12 Months)

  • Develop a minimum viable product (MVP)
  • Launch pilot programs in select schools
  • Measure learning outcomes and engagement
  • Onboard early team members
  • Begin fundraising conversations

Phase 4: Expansion & Growth (12+ Months)

  • Scale to additional schools and regions
  • Expand feature set based on learnings
  • Build partnerships with government programs
  • Develop sustainable business model
  • Grow the team with experienced professionals
