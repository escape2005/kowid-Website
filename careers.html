Careers: Build With Us

Join Kowid. Build something that matters.

Join Kowid. Build Something That Matters.

Early-stage. Messy. Rewarding. Not for everyone—but maybe for you.

🚧 We're Early. Really Early.

No polished office, no clear roadmap. You'll figure things out as much as execute them.

👔 You'll Wear Many Hats

Research, prototype, pitch—all in one day. Things outside your job description? That's the norm.

💡 Your Ideas Shape the Product

We're building the vision together. Your insights directly influence what we become.

📈 You'll Learn Fast

Experience in months that might take years elsewhere. Fail, iterate, grow faster than you imagined.

❤️ You'll Work With People Who Care

Everyone here could be doing something easier. We're here because education matters.

What We Value

Curiosity – You ask "why?" and "what if?"
Ownership – You don't wait to be told what to do
Resilience – You handle ambiguity and setbacks with grace
Empathy – You care about the people we're building for
Humility – You listen, learn, and stay open to being wrong
Execution – You ship, even when it's imperfect

What We Offer

We're honest: we can't compete with big tech on salary—yet. But we offer:

  • Equity and ownership in what we're building
  • Flexibility and autonomy
  • Direct impact on a mission that matters
  • A culture of learning and growth
  • The chance to build something from the ground up
